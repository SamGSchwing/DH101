<p>This is an embedded .html file that runs my game! Really cool! My game is about a person who has to navigate an interview, but the interviewer is AI. It is meant to be a funny game that pokes fun at the coldness of AI and the matter of factness that its responses usually have. The dry humor of the choices make the game fun. The choices matter because they influence the ending that you receive. The mechanics are very basic, you get to choose between two options, but that is just the limitation of working with twine. It does work well with an interview type game because it is all text based so there is no need to worry about sounds or images.
</p>

<div class="card-section">
<h2>Q and A</h2>

<p><strong>What did I learn?</strong><br>
I learned that games are actually really easy to make with online tools! I actually had a really fun time working with Twine, even though I was a little bit worried because I had never tried to make a text based game before and was worried it would be out of my technical scope. The way I used AI made it even more accessible. I wrote out a story that I wanted to be told along with 90% of the used dialogue and had the AI put it in a copy-pastable format that I could really easily put into the website. If i had to make a new game I would love to make it about something unrelated to AI because I feel like it really limits our creativity. I think a game about exploring a dark forest would be really fun to write and make. 
</p>
