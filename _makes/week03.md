<h2>Reflection</h2>
<p>

I came up with the idea to make a funny comic based off of a meme I saw on instagram. The original meme had someone “invent” a paper pan to cook food on. I asked AI to create a comic that captured this idea as well and it drafted a few story ideas. The one that I settled on was a pair of techy shoes that were actually just a big downgrade from any actual shoes. The AI was good at capturing the cartoony look that I was going for but fell apart in every aspect relating to the dialogue. The dialogue that it recommended felt choppy and often repeated lines. It had the shoe saying lines that were meant for the other characters and nonsensical things. This is where I had to do the most interfering, rewriting the dialogue over and over, even when I gave very specific instructions I could not get the dialogue to sound well written and I eventually ran out of the free image generations on DALLE. This made me think about the way that AI interprets our prompts, it doesn’t really understand exactly what we are looking for, but it spits out a product that it thinks matches our given specifications.</p>
