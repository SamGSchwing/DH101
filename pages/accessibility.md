# Accessibility

This site was built by Sam Schwing for DH 101. 

 

I have tried to make it accessible by:

 

- Adding a site tutorial! It can be accessed through hitting the site tour button in the top left when you first open the page. 
- I made the buttons very big and eye catching.
- The index has big cards to make text very easy to read.
